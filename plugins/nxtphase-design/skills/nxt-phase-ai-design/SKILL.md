Core rules to honor every time: warm cream surfaces (never pure white), near-black ink, **one** Signal Green accent, at most one supporting color per section; PP Editorial New for headlines, Switzer for everything else, max two weights; uppercase only for 0.12em overlines/labels; soft generous radii (never pills); warm restrained shadows; calm ~200ms motion, no bounce; dot-based icons (espresso on light, green when active) — never emoji. *Braun, not Apple. Editorial, not startup.*
